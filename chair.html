What is Traverse?

Engle Dental Systems has the only Traverse Chair on the market.

Traverse helps maximize your operator space and ensures perfect patient positioning from the start. Traverse enables Zero Clearance so as the patient reclines, the head rest moves straight down.

  • Optimized for smaller operatory
  • More room for rear delivery
  • Perfect patient positioning from the start
  • Smart chair movement

Precise Motion
